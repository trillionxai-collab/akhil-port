I focused on applied AI for automation, natural language processing and computer vision, building systems that improved manufacturing workflows and quality control. This experience sharpened my expertise in designing intelligent solutions, scaling AI models, and creating practical automation pipelines for real-world industrial problems.

Key learnings: advanced AI research, automation architecture, NLP applications, and vision-based intelligence for production-grade systems.

Workshops for Future Engineers — Empowering Young Minds

After quitting from Samsung, I realized how far the Indian education system is from real industry needs. Students despite studying engineering degrees somehow they end up in bank jobs.

They were taught to memorize, not to build. I wanted to make them understand that "the best way to learn, is to do". So by partnering with Codesamaaj, a tech community founded by my friend, I leaded pratical workshops and bootcamps. Where we work together to build real working projects from the scratch. Which was the missing part of their college studies yet was the crucial part of the ENGINEERING Journey.

One of my student said: — He gets to the part where it matters. the logic of the code. like he said, anyone can get the code, but no one can understand for us. its important to understand why and how the code works. he taught us that.
